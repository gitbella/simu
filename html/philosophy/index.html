Investment Philosophy－HE JU INVESTMENT has always pursued value investment dynamically

We believe that 1) the Law of Value remains the fundamental principle dictating the Chinese securities market, 2) Listed companies’ intrinsic value dictates the long term trend for their share prices while external environment exert noticeable influence on pace and pattern of share price movement. However, the Law of Value is embodied distinctly in the A-share market. To generate excess returns, one thus has to know well the basic characteristics of this market and pursue value investment dynamically.

We are convinced that one can uncover attractive investments with the help of quality research. Having focused on China’s industrial regime and cycles as well as the rules by which its capital market runs, we have been looking to identify stocks with values that are being recognized by the market, underpinned by our team’s superior research and valuation capabilities. We have built up HE JU INVESTMENT’s research strengths and investment achievements through sharing knowledge and pooling resources.

Basic Strategy

HE JU INVESTMENT has been upgrading both of its aggressive and defensive portfolio strategies.

Go aggressive: Stick to value investment and generate Alpha through active management

Based on value investment, we have been looking for undervalued investment targets actively with the help of intensive fundamental research. We have balanced our aggressive investment portfolio by picking the best ones among the shortlisted pool of stocks, which enables us to generate Alpha.

Go defensive: employing multiple strategies to reduce volatility in NAV

Under this strategy, we have attached importance to diversification amongst various asset classes and prudently estimate specific stocks’ margin of safety. Given the highly volatile nature of the A-share market, we have adopted multiple strategies and various trading tools in order to minimize the correlation between the NAV and the market.

Stock Picking Strategy－HE JU INVESTMENT has been looking for robust companies in sectors.

1.​Identifying major investment fields in a top-down method

Figure out the trends for and rhythms of various asset classes and the rule on rotation. Identify major investment fields in a top-down approach by looking for cyclical stocks on their up-phase and growth stocks with sustainability during an economic transition.

2.​Pick stocks in a bottom-up method

Time investments through comprehensive research and in-depth company assessment, coupled with market sentiment. Spot out major investment targets by extensive research and decisive execution. In terms of investment targets, we have focused on growth stocks and turnaround stocks.
