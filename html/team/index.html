Investment Team

  • YU Jun · Executive Director

    With a broad vision in equity research, Mr. YU is adept at capturing the timing of investments into major asset classes with incisive decision-making. He had been awarded Best Research Analyst in the poll sponsored by New Fortune (a Chinese magazine on par with Institutional Investor or Aisamoney) for four consecutive years and boasts abundant research resources.

    Experience

    With a Ph.D in Economics, Mr. YU has worked in the securities sector for over two decades. Before joining HE JU INVESTMENT as the Chairman of Investment Committee in 2012, he was Executive Director, Chief Transportation Analyst and Chief Strategist at the Research Department of CITICS. 

  • LI Zegang · General Manager

    Mr. LI is known for his ability in picking stocks. He attaches great importance to margin of safety in stock selection, balanced allocation while structuring a portfolio and looking for robust companies in sectors in terms of the research process.

    Experience

    Mr. LI received a Master in Management from Fudan University and a Bachelor in Economics from Nankai University. He has 14 years of experience in funds management and was a portfolio manager of ABN AMRO TEDA Fund Management. He is a veteran in sector research and company valuation. He established BEIJING HEJU INVESTMENT in 2009.

  • JI Lu · Director of Private Placement Investment

    Prior to joining HE JU INVESTMENTHE in April 2016, Mr. Ji was a Vice President of Investment Banking Division, Director of Capital Market and Director of Senior Clients in Minsheng Securities Co., Ltd. Prior to Minsheng Securities, Mr. Ji spent 10 years in First Capital Securities Co. Ltd where he served as the

    Head of Sales and Trading Department. As a veteran investment banker, Mr. Ji oversaw IPOs and private placements for over 40 issuers.

    Mr. Ji holds a Master from Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics. 

  • FAN Xiaohui · Director of Institutional Business

    Mr. Fan has 9 years of experience in the securities industry (3 years of research in H-shares and 6 years in A-shares). He worked at Hong Kong BOCOM International and the research department of Orient Securities Company as research analyst and institutional salesperson. He studied in the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign for 1 year. He has a deep understanding of the international investment market, including H-shares, US stocks and A-shares. He once engaged in the field of financial investment which includes but not limited to institutional sales and investment research, from which he obtained extensive experience. He is well-connected and has forged broad cooperation with main securities companies, institutional investors and listed companies in China.

    Mr. Fan received a Master in Public Finance from Yunnan University of Finance and Economics. 

  • CHEN Jian · Chief Analyst

    Mr. Chen has been in the securities industry since 2010 and was most recently working as the Chief Telecom Analyst of CITIC Securities. Prior to that, Mr. Chen worked at China Telecom’s strategy department for 7 years. Mr. Chen ranked among the top in Best Telecom Analyst poll by New Fortune , Capital Week as well as several other polls by  Wind, CBN, Reuters, etc. Mr. Chen maintains forward thinking in his research over industry chains, which involves internet infrastructure (IDC, CDN, cloud computing and big data), Internet of Things, Internet Plus, Enterprise-Grade Network, wireless devices, optical communications and telecommunications companies both domestic and international.

    Mr. Chen holds a Master  in Management and a Bachelor in Material science from Tsinghua University.

  • MAI Turong · Chief Analyst

    Mr. Mai has 6 years' experience in securities and Chemical industries, respectively. He worked as the chief analyst for the basic chemical sector at Shenwan Hongyuan Securities, and analyst for Cinda Securities’ research and development center. He ranked the third place in the Best Petrochemical Analyst poll by New Fortune, and was forerunner in Best Chemical Analyst poll. He specializes in combining industrial analysis with investment, exploring investment opportunities from the perspective of industry chain. Besides, he has a deep understanding of the investment rationale for cyclical and growth themes. His investment research includes energy and chemical materials sectors. He has an intimate knowledge of petroleum, coal and natural gas industry chain, and covers chemical fertilizer, agricultural chemicals, textile chemicals, fine chemical intermediates, new energies, automotive light weight, methanol economy, and carbon emission fields.

    Mr. MAI holds a Master in Economics from Peking University.

  • LI Zhiwu · Chief Analyst

    Mr. LI is one of HE JU INVESTMENT’s Chief Analysts. H mainly cover telecommunication sector and small-& mid-caps. Prior to joining HE JU INVESTMENT, Mr. LI served as a senior analyst in Essence Securities for 3 years and BOCOM Research for 8 years. He was well known for identifying undervalued small and mid-caps, which successfully generated considerable returns. Considering his research achievement, Mr. LI was awarded as the “Golden Finger”, “Stock-picking Master” by the mainstream media of Hong Kong, and the interview about him was published on IMONEY as a feature story subsequently. Additionally, Mr. LI  ranked the second place in Best China Small and Mid-cap Analysts as well as several nominations in polls by  Institutional Investor and Asiamoney.

    Mr. Li holds a master degree in national economics from Nanjing University.

  • ZOU Xuyuan · Chief Analyst

    Mr. ZOU has 12 years' experience in securities research. He once was with the research department of China Galaxy Securities Co., Ltd. as the chief analyst in public utilities, electric power equipment as well as new energy sectors and later Executive Director. He ranked among the top in Best  Public Utilities Analyst poll by New Fortune and the Best Analyst poll by Capital Week.

    Mr. ZOU holds a Master of Science in Finance and Investment from University of Exeter.
