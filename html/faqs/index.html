Private Equity Funds
What are Privately Equity Funds?

Private equity funds refer to the investment funds established by raising capitals from qualified institutional or retail investors in a non-public manner within the territory of the People's Republic of China.

The identification of a qualified investor.

According to the Interim Measures for the Supervision and Administration of Private Equity Investment Fund, a qualified investor of Private Equity Funds shall refer to an entity or individual that has the appropriate capability of risk identification and risk tolerance, invests at least Rmb1mn  in a single Private equity Fund, and meets any of the following standards:    
(1) A qualified investor, if it is an entity, shall have net assets no less than Rmb10mn; or  
(2) A qualified investor, if being a natural person, shall have financial assets no less than Rmb3mn or average annual personal income no less than Rmb500,000 in the past three years.   For the purpose of the preceding paragraph, financial assets shall include bank deposits, stocks, bonds, managed funds, asset management schemes, bank wealth management products, trust schemes, insurance products, futures etc.  

How can I choose a good private equity fund manager?

1. To ensure the safety of money, investors should examine the qualification of a fund management institution as well as the fund manager who will ultimately manage the investment. Generally, private equity fund management companies will undergo the fund recordation formalities with the securities regulatory authority. Additionally, a qualified fund custodian will be appointed for safekeeping of the fund assets, such as a commercial bank or any other financial institution. Investors should never invest with unqualified private fund management companies in a bid to protect their interests.  
2. The research capability and risk controls rules of a private equity fund management company are also a key point to check. The track record of a private fund manager throughout the entire market cycles plays an important role when considering the consistency of fund performance.
3. Investors are recommended to understand the private equity fund managers’ investment philosophy, strategies and styles. Generally, a seasoned fund manager, with an investment philosophy founded on extensive investment experience, is more likely to generate consistent returns in the long run. Meanwhile, investors are suggested to make investment decisions based on their risk appetite and investment styles.
4. Furthermore, a thorough understanding of the financial market will facilitate investment decisions in accordance with investment styles.

About customer service
After investing into products managed by BEIJING HE JU INVESTMENT, how can I check the net asset value and changes to unit holdings?

To check the net asset value of the fund and changes to unit holdings, clients can obtain those information through: 
1. The official website of HE JU INVESTMENT.
2. The official websites of related private equity fund management companies and  fund custodians.
3. The official WeChat Public Account of HE JU. With a registered phone number, customers can check the net asset value as well as the changes to unit holdings by accessing HE JU’s official WeChat Public Account. For more information, please dial 400-618-2112.

How often does HE JU INVESTMENT update the net asset value of the fund under management?

Based on information released each Friday, the NAV of the fund will be updated on the following Wednesday at HE JU’s official website and WeChat Public Account simultaneously.

What is the process of subscription and redemption?

Investors should submit application with supplementary materials ten working days prior the fund offering starts. A fund contract will be formed when an investor pays for the fund units to which the investor subscribes. For fund redemption, the fund holder should submit two copies of the Redemption Request Form ten working days prior to the fund opening day which will be received and confirmed by the fund manager. The subscription and redemption will be considered as valid once it is confirmed by the fund manager, except as otherwise agreed upon in the fund contract.

Login Instructions
I have invested into your funds, why can’t I register and log in?

According to the requirements in “The Measures for Information Disclosure Management” published by the Asset Management Association of China, the product information can only be disclosed to shareholders of relevant products, i.e., customers must perform the registration process before log in. Please use your authentic information to complete the registration. In case of registration failure, please contact our Client Service Representatives on 4006182112.

I have not invested into your products, can I register on the website?

According to the requirements in “The Measures for Information Disclosure Management” published by the Asset Management Association of China, the product information can only be disclosed to shareholders of relevant products. Therefore, customers who are not investors of the fund cannot register or log in on the website.

What kind of services do you provide after registration?

When registered, clients can log onto the official website of BEIJING HE JU INVESMENT to check the net asset value of the fund, public announcements and investment strategies. (Not including the funds which are managed by Trustee Companies and fund Subsidiary companies)
