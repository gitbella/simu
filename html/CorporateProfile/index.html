Corporate Profile

Established in 2009, BEIJING HE JU INVESTMENT is one of the leading private equity companies in China. In 2013, HE JU INVESTMENT was registered as a financial institution with Asset Management Association of China and in turn became one of the first special members of the Association. HE JU INVESTMENT has forged an active and progressive investment style which integrates both contrarian and momentum strategies into the Chinese capital market.

With FUM of over Rmb10bn and focusing on the Chinese securities market, HEJU INVESTMENT has maintained an outstanding investment record over the long term and been well acknowledged by Chinese and overseas fund rating agencies. It has won almost all mainstream accolades in the securities sector, including Golden Bull Award (five times), Golden Sunshine Award, Golden Goblet Award, Yinghua Award (nicknamed as the Academy Award for portfolio managers), Award for products with a tenure of 3-5 years on the Annual Conference of China Private Equity Fund etc. In 2015, it was the only institution awarded the China Fund Prize by MorningStar (for equity hedge fund).

Having dug extensively into the Chinese capital market, HE JU INVESTMENT has come up with innovative asset management businesses continually. With deregulation of the private equity industry, HE JU INVESTMENT is witnessing unprecedented development opportunities. We have incorporated various trading tools and varieties of investment into our product strategies, including private placements, derivatives, inter-market strategies etc., in a bid to continually extract investment opportunities in the Chinese capital market.

Corporate Vision

HE JU INVESTMENT is committed to becoming a leading investment house in China. We have generated outstanding investment returns for our clients and offer a premium platform for our employees with professional investment and research teams, distinctive investment style, strict risk management, superior corporate governance and attractive long-term incentive mechanism.

Corporate Culture

HE JU INVESTMENT provides a culture of diligence, professionalism and harmony. An elite investment team, loyal clients and reliable partners are the foundations of our business as well as giving us the confidence to pursue the highest of the standards.

We don’t believe in success by chance. HE JU INVESTMENT has been following increasingly stringent standards. We persevere with a diligent and prudent attitude, endorsing both prudent decision-making and innovative thinking. We look for the best talents for our research and investment teams, respect individual values and cherish teamwork. We always place investor interests at the top of our priority and pursue long-term absolute return for them with risks well controlled. We also endorse cooperation with partners to keep building on our success stories together.
